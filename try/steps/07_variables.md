## Variables

Stylus variables behave as you would expect in any other language,
and may optionally be prefixed by the "$" character:

<div><textarea class="stylus">
#prompt
  position: absolute
  top: 150px
  left: 50%
  width: w = 200px
  margin-left: -(w / 2)
</textarea></div>
</div>
